ℹ️ About Hinglish Secret Code

Hinglish Secret Code is a simple yet powerful tool designed to encrypt your messages into a unique coded format to keep your conversations private. It ensures that **only the intended person can decode** and read your messages.

🔍 Features

  • 🔹 Custom Encryption Algorithm – No one else can guess your encoded messages.
  • 🔹 Fast Encoding & Decoding – Convert messages instantly.
  • 🔹 Works Offline – No internet required for secure communication.
  • 🔹 Easy to Use – Just type, encode, and share!

📌 Who Can Use This?

  • ✔ Friends & Couples – Secretly chat without worrying.
  • ✔ Students – Share coded messages during class 😉.
  • ✔ Privacy Enthusiasts – Keep your messages safe from prying eyes.
